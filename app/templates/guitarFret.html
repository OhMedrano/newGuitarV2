How to use
Hit the controls tab, and pick between either the tuning or scales on the left side.

On the right, the different options to customize your tunings or pick your scales, will appear.

The scale themselves are color coded by scale interval names, for you music theory folks.

Change the notations by selecting the Flats or Sharps tab in the controls menu.

Change for either Right-handed or Left-handed versions (I care about you lefties.)
Why use it
I made this because I was tired of trying to remember where my scales fell in a different tuning.

Figured it could be useful for other people so I decided to expand on it a bit more.

This is made for the guitarist who has problems visualizing the notes on the fretboard, from beginner to experts.
